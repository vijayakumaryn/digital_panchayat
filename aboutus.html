Digital Panchayat

The Commissioner Panchayati Raj and Rural Development has initiated Digital Panchayat, a flagship G2C project, for computerization and online issuance of citizen-centric certificates. After a detailed system study by NIC, the department prioritized the applications to be automated for gram panchayats. The application areas include:

  • Birth & Death Registrations
  • House Tax Demand Collections
  • Trade Licenses, Property Valuation, Mutations
  • Water Tap Connections, NOCs, Building Permissions, Layout Permissions
  • MNREGA Management and Reporting

These applications provide an end-to-end solution for all Gram Panchayats in the state. The software facilitates certificate generation, payment gateway for citizens, eSign facility for panchayat functionaries, SMS and email alerts. The computerization of all three tiers of PRIs, i.e., Zilla Praja Parishad, Mandal Praja Parishad (Block), and Gram Panchayats is also in place.
